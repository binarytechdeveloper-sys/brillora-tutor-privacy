Privacy Policy

Brillora Tutor App

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Brillora Tutor is an educational application designed for teachers and tutors to manage classrooms, students, courses, tests, live classes, and announcements. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details such as name, email address, profile photo
  • Account and authentication information
  • Classroom, course, test, and student management data
  • Communication data such as messages and notifications
  • Device and usage information for app performance and security

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide core app features and services
  • Manage classrooms, students, and educational content
  • Enable communication between tutors and students
  • Improve app performance, security, and user experience
  • Send important notifications related to your account

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data may be shared only with trusted third-party services required to operate the app, such as:

  • Firebase (authentication, database, analytics)
  • Payment service providers (for tutor payments, if applicable)
  • Cloud services for notifications and storage

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data. While no system is completely secure, we take reasonable measures to prevent unauthorized access or misuse.

6. Children’s Privacy

Brillora Tutor is intended for educators. Student data is managed by tutors and used strictly for educational purposes. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children without proper authorization.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us. Account deletion requests will be handled according to applicable laws and platform policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
